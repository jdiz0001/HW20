Connect with the right buddy with eBuddy

Melt away the awkward tension with eBuddy by connecting with the right buddy.

The Problem: The main problem we have observed is that active social media users have spent a lot of time and effort struggling to search for specific interest matches online. Apart from the main problem, we have also noticed that people worry about their online security and feel awkward to engage with fresh faces for the first time online.

The Solution: A mobile app named eBuddy is therefore designed to help mentally exhausted active social media users effectively and efficiently connect to people who share commonalities such as the same interest with them online. For example, allowing people to filter out individuals based on shared common interests. We have also designed the user identity verification measure and in-app ice-breaker games to address the other problems raised by our participants.
